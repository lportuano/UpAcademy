Nivel B2: Upper-Int

Expressing Complexity

Aprende a argumentar con fluidez y usar condicionales avanzados.

01. Conditionals: 2nd & 3rd

Hypothetical Present (2nd)

"If I had more time, I would study for a Master's degree."

Past Regrets (3rd)

"If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam."

02. Passive Voice for Formal Reports

Essential for professional and academic contexts where the action is more important than the subject.

Active

"The company launched a new app."

Passive (B2)

"A new app was launched by the company."

Persuasion & Debate

Stating Opinion:
"From my point of view, the benefits outweigh the risks."
Disagreeing Politely:
"I see your point, but I'm afraid I have a different take."
Adding Points:
"On top of that, we should consider the environmental impact."

B2 Tip!

Stop using "I think". Try "I reckon" or "It is my firm belief".
