Phrasal Verbs for Success

Scale up

To increase in size or importance.

"We need to scale up production."

Iron out

To resolve small details or problems.

"Let's iron out the contract details."

Draw up

To prepare a formal document/plan.

"The lawyers will draw up the agreement."

Advanced Idioms & Expressions

01

"To cut a long story short"

Used when you omit details to get to the point.

02

"The bottom line is..."

The most important fact in a situation (financial or otherwise).

03

"Beat around the bush"

Avoid talking about what is important.

Mastering Advanced Grammar: Negative Inversion

At C1 level, you must use inversion to emphasize points in formal writing.

// Normal Structure

"I have never seen such a professional presentation."

// Inverted Structure (C1)

"Never have I seen such a professional presentation."
